Air Fryer Honey Garlic Chicken Wings

These chicken wings are the perfect amount crispy and juicy. Easy to make and great for a get together.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 pounds chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger root
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 2 tablespoons green onions

Steps

  1. Preheat airfryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Mix baking powder, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and cayenne pepper together in a small bowl.
  3. Pat chicken ings dry with paper towels, then toss them in spice mixture until evenly coated. Arrange wings in a single layer in the air fryer basket.
  4. Cook wings in the preheated aur fryer until cripsy and golden brown, juices are clear, and meat is no longer pink at the bone, 20 to 25 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 265 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  5. Meanwhile, make the honey garlic sauce. Stir honey, soy souce, garlic, and ginger together in a small saucepan over medium heat.
  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together cornstarch and water until smooth, then add to the saucepan. Cook until sauce thickens, 1 to 2 minutes.
  7. Transer wings to a large bowl, pour honey garlic sauce over wings, and toss to coat evenly. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.
